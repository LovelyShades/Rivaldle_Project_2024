About Us
Rivaldle was created by two computer science students passionate about programming and eager to deepen their skills through a collaborative project. As lifelong Marvel fans, we’ve spent countless hours enjoying Marvel Rivals, both together and with friends, which inspired us to develop a fun trivia game centered on the Marvel universe.
We also drew inspiration from Smashdle, an engaging online trivia game based on Super Smash Bros, combining our love for gaming and interactive challenges. With Rivaldle, our aim is to create an enjoyable and immersive experience for fans who share our enthusiasm.
Ultimately, our goal is to provide a fun and engaging platform where Marvel enthusiasts can test their knowledge and celebrate their fandom.

How To Play
Rivaldle is a Marvel Rivals based trivia game with the objective being to guess the correct character in each of the respective modes
As of now Rivaldle featured four modes. Classic, Silhouetter, Emoji, and Character Ability. Each of these modes provides a different method of guessing the correct character for that mode
Each of these modes can be compleated at any time by clicking the desired mode box on the home page.
Example:
Mode Box
More information on the individual modes will be avalable on their respective pages.
Each one of these modes features a different character with them changing daily. When the characters reset, the new character is chosen randomly with no consideration of the previous days character
