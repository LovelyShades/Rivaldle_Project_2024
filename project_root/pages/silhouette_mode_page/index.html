How to Play Rivaldle: Silhouette Mode
1. Understanding the Gameplay
In this mode, you will be given a silhouette of a Marvel character to start. Your goal is to guess the mystery character based on the clues provided. Each time you make a wrong guess, the silhouette will zoom out slightly, revealing more of the character, and helping you get closer to identifying the correct answer.
2. Interpreting Silhouette
After each guess, the photo zooms out slightly, giving more clues on the character's appearance. Here are some things to consider:
  • Body Shape: Look for general body type and posture. For example, a large, muscular silhouette may be Hulk, while a more agile, slimmer shape could be Spider-Man.
  • Clothing Shape: Pay attention to the style or shape of their outfit. A distinctive cape could hint at Moon Knight, or a sleek body suit might suggest Black Widow.
  • Hair Shape: Notice the style or length of hair. A ponytail silhouette might represent Psylocke, while long, flowing hair could be a clue for Scarlet Witch.
  • Accessories or Gear: Items like a bow (Hawkeye) or a sword (Magik) might be visible even in the silhouette.
  • Tail or Ears: Characters like Squirrel Girl (with her squirrel ears) or a character with an animalistic tail like Rocket could be identified by those features.
